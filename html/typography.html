Emphasis

Make use of tags <strong>, <small>, and <em> for additional styles. <strong> formemphasising a snippet of text with a heavier font-weight. <small> for de-emphasising inline or blocks of text. <em> for emphasising a snippet of text with italics.

Abbreviations

Stylized implementation of HTML's <abbr> element for abbreviations and acronyms to show the expanded version on hover. Abbreviations with a <title> attribute have a light dotted bottom border and a help cursor on hover, providing additional context on hover.

An abbreviation of Hyper Text Markup Language is HTML.
